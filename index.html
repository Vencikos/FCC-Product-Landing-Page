Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro 2020

  • 15.6-inch screen, 1920x1080 pixel display.
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-10210U or i7-10510U processor, Quad core up to 4.9GHz.
  • GPU: 2G GDDR5 NVIDIA GeForce MX350 Discrete Graphics.

  • Windows 10 Home operating system

Features

10th Generation Intel Core i5-10210U Quad Core Up to 4.2GHz or i7-10510U Quad Core Up to 4.9GHz

Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro 15 2020 is equipped with the 10th generation Intel Core i5 or i7-10510U processor, the highest core frequency can reach 4.9GHz, and the overall CPU performance is 7%-10% higher than that of the 8th generation.

15.6 inch FHD IPS Screen with 1920 x 1080 Resolution

Offer good experience for watching videos and browsing the Web. 1920 x 1080p resolution (142 ppi), 178° wide angle, 300 Nit, Contrast ratio of 800 : 1, widescreen 16 : 9, wide display angle, 100% sRGB high color gamut, fully laminated to reduce glare.

2GB GDDR5 NVIDIA GeForce MX350 Discrete Graphics

Dual graphics supported, better and faster in playing games and watching videos

8GB/16GB DDR4 RAM for Advanced Multitasking

Substantial high-bandwidth RAM to smoothly run your games, photos and video-editing applications

512GB/1TB PCIe ×4 NVMe SSD Storage Capacity

Provides room to store pictures, videos, music and more

Dual Heat Pipe Cooling System

Dual Heat Pipe Cooling System is upgraded from the single heat pipe of Xiaomi Notebook Pro 15.6" 2019, dual heat pipe and double fan, larger heat conduction area, efficient and rapid cooling.

Fingerprint Unlock

Unlock the touchpad with fingerprint, wake up to desktop in 2 seconds. It supports Xiaomi sensor key function, close to the sensorless unlock, and the computer automatically locks the screen when leaving.

7 Major Interfaces

Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro 15 2020 has seven interfaces. With the Xiaomi multi-function adapter, it can expand the USB interface, HDMI interface, VGA interface, Mini DP interface, and Gigabit network port.

60W Battery Supports Fast Charging

60W (typ) ultra thin and light battery, 4-cell lithium ion battery, Battery energy density up to 578Wh/L, Supports 1C fast charge, charges 50% in 40 minutes, USB Type-C power adapter port. Online video playback up to 15 hours, Local video playback up to 17 hours, Web browsing time up to 12 hours.
